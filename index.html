Understanding PRIMACARE

10 Things to help you fully understand primacare & become a member

1. BENEFITS OF FULL MEMBERSHIP

Get access to all the repairs and maintenance your car deserves. the only requirement is the renewal of your monthly premium. When the route to membership is complete, users become members and gain full access to all the car care you need & primacare provides.

2. REGISTRATION FEE & WHAT IT COVERS

If you want to become a member, the first step is to register. Be aware registration comes with a fee. From the moment of registration to full membership and beyond data as related and determined by your car's make and model is collected, analyzed and disbursed to the relevant parties. the data helps facilitate communication with OEMs, assign specialized technicians, creation of inventory as related to you and assign a repository, tech support & more.

3. WHAT ARE MEMBERSHIP ROUTES

The routes to memberships are different but the goal is the same. The route system allows us to offer a subsidized version of the service while protecting our interests. While on the membership route, there are limitations placed on certain services, this helps users appreciate the benefits of full membership offers. Pick the route that works for you and at the pace that best suits your needs.

4. REGISTER (SLOW TRACK) VS FAST TRACK

Each route to membership has an estimated period. Users can follow the paced track which takes longer to get membership and the data collected are more on a user base (When the service is used). users can also opt for the fast track which is shorter route to membership, The data collection is narrowed down to the peculiarities of your make and model. paced track allows you to use the service in its limited form while fast track would require a little patience as the team constructs a management style for you according to your car, global data & and other inferences such as OEM, Inventory creation, stock management, etc. i.e fast track does not offer the service in its limited form but runs through the checklist & allows you to get to membership quicker and unlock the full benefits of primacare.

5. MONTHS I DO NOT USE THE SERVICE BUT MY PREMIUM IS PAID?

As required keep your membership active and your inventory number allocated for parts & and services. Members are required to keep their premiums up to date. We love our cars, months were the premium was not exercised we convert it to inventory stock points, this helps us facilitate an open connection with our OEMs to show the value of our members. This points showcase our members as high priorty targets whenever parts are in shortage or circulation.

6. REFERRAL PROGRAMS

From the road to membership and becoming a full member. Referral programs can be beneficial to both users and members. Referral points can be redeemed for car necessities, from accessories from the store, points to pay off the next membership premium, to the purchase of fuel/disease amongst other benefits. We offer referral links at the point of registration.

7. WHAT ARE REPAIR CHARGES?

The benefits of being a member are access to repairs and service at a level unparallel to what you would get in the traditional market. Repair charges are the price users pay on the route to membership for repairs & and spare parts used. The charges are subsidized but still far from the the full benefits members enjoy. charges happen when a repair has occurred/parts replaced.

8. WHAT ARE SESSIONS?

The number of times in a month users can gain services on the route to membership should you choose the slow route

9. PRIORITY ATTENTION?

Users may schedule time for repairs and other services. We assign technicians specialized to handle that car make and model to attend to the user. Should a mechanic/technician not be available or scheduled, users can declare a high-priority claim which attracts a fee and receive the necessary attention swiftly.

10. WHERE CAN I FIND PRIMACARE?

Primacare's model is simple, we arre mobie. We are equipped to handle your issues at anytime, anywhere. Quality service when you need it, where you need it. Regardless we have worked a physical plan into our model. We partner with well equipped workshops for moments when your car needs that extra touch of attention
