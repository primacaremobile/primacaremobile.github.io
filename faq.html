Frequently Asked Questions

Popular Questions

  • 1. What is PrimaCare?
    PrimaCare is a subscription-based mobile auto mechanic service designed to provide convenient and reliable car maintenance and repairs.
  • 2. How do I sign up?
    You can sign up by clicking the sign up button anywhere on the site, paying a one-time registration fee of ₦10,000 and a monthly subscription fee of ₦25,000. There is a sign up button at the bottom of the FAQ
  • 3. What services are included in the subscription?
    Our subscription covers all your car mechanic needs except for accidents and third-party repairs.we will not cover purchase of oil for oil changes until the first year of subscriptions have elapsed. we will not cover paint work until first 9 months have elapsed, we will not cover repairs of over ₦40,000 until the third month of active subscription. the reason for this being, we will create an inventory calendar for your cars make and model, while collecting data to ensure that optimal service is provided to your with records unique to you.
  • 4. What if my car needs a major repair?
    Major repairs are also covered under our comprehensive plan. Our team will assess the situation and provide you with the best solution to get your vehicle back on the road as soon as possible. we will investigate repairs to help you maintain and improve the lifespan and mileage of your car therefore we will not attend to self sabotaged cars, manipulated, fraudulent or restoration attempt cars
  • 5. Why is there a registration fee?
    The registration fee covers the Initial Setup Costs, Inspections & documentations, Personalized service plan creation, quality assurance, Inventory Creation, Professional Scrutiny on your cars make and model, mileage & usage
  • 6. Are there additional charges?
    Yes, there are additional charges for roadside assistance (₦4,000) and priority attention bookings (₦6,000). No charges on scheduled Appointments
  • 7. Can I register more than one car?
    Yes, each car requires its own subscription plan.
  • 8. What are your operating hours?
    We operate from 8 AM to 7 PM on weekdays and 9 AM to 3 PM on Saturdays. We are closed on Sundays. Appointments booked outside of operation hours may have extra charges if approved
  • 9. Do you offer accident repairs?
    We will assist with substantial accident repairs but reserve the right to deny any that may infringe on insurance regulations as we reframe from branding as an insurance provider.
  • 10. Where are you located?

While thrieving for bigger Collaborations and pop up shops were needed. Our administrative offices are as stated at the end of the page should anyone require assistance
  • 11. Why should I keep my subscription up to date?
    Keeping your subscription current ensures that all data related to your vehicle is accurately maintained and matched to you as the owner. This helps in enhancing the lifespan of your car through timely and precise maintenance and support.
  • 12. What happens if I skip a month's subscription?
    Skipping a month's subscription can lead to delays in the dispatch of resources for your vehicle. This is because our system matches records of inventory, stock, and active personnel to the car's make and model. If your subscription lapses, the necessary resources might be allocated to another vehicle of the same make and model, causing delays in servicing your car.
  • 13. How does my subscription help in managing resources for my car?
    Your active subscription allows us to efficiently manage and dispatch the required resources for your car based on its specific make and model. This includes timely maintenance, parts replacement, and access to skilled personnel. Without an up-to-date subscription, we cannot guarantee the prompt availability of these resources.
  • 14. Will my car’s data be affected if I miss a subscription payment?
    Yes, missing a subscription payment can affect how data is matched to your vehicle. Our system relies on up-to-date subscriptions to ensure that all records are correctly linked to the car owner. A lapse in your subscription might result in incomplete or delayed updates to your vehicle’s maintenance and service records.
  • 15. Can I reinstate my subscription if it lapses?
    Yes, you can reinstate your subscription if it lapses. However, there might be a delay in updating your vehicle's data and in the allocation of resources, as we would need to reprocess the information and ensure all necessary resources are available.
  • 16. How does my subscription benefit the lifespan of my car?
    An active subscription ensures that your vehicle receives regular maintenance and timely support, which are crucial for its longevity. By keeping your subscription up to date, you ensure that your car gets the best possible care and attention, which can significantly enhance its lifespan.
  • 17. What should I do if I am unable to maintain my subscription for a month?
    If you anticipate difficulties in maintaining your subscription for a month, please contact our customer support team. They may be able to provide options or assistance to ensure that your vehicle’s service and maintenance schedules are not disrupted.
  • 18. How do I update or manage my subscription?
    You can update or manage your subscription through our online portal or by contacting our customer support team. They can assist you with any changes or updates you need to make to your subscription plan.
  • 19. Who can I contact for more information or assistance with my subscription?
